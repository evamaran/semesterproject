Visit Us

Location

The museum is located at Scholars Avenue 100, 1250 University Park.

Parking

Free parking in front of the museum. EV chargers on the north side (fees apply).

Hours

Monday: Closed

Thursday - Friday: 10:00 - 18:00

Saturday: 9:00 - 16:00

Sunday: 9:00 - 13:00

Admission

Entrance is free for all. Guided tours every hour: 70 NOK per person including printed guide. For groups of 8+, contact us to arrange a tour.

Accessibility

Wheelchair ramps, audio guides, and braille display signs available throughout the museum.

Food & Drink

Our café offers light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Explore memorabilia, gifts and activity packs to continue your science journey at home.
