For Teachers

As a community-driven museum, we collaborate with schools to create places of learning and exploration. Our team is ready to welcome your students with guided tours, hands-on experiments in our learning lab, and engaging video presentations that spark curiosity and excitement.

Get in touch with us to learn more about our facilities and to arrange a visit tailored to your class.
